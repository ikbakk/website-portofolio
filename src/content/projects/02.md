title: 'Beaconnect - reassurance-first companion app'
---

The constraint that shaped it: help two people feel connected without
making either person feel watched.

I built a Flutter companion app around mutual pairing, deliberate check-ins,
place snapshots, live sharing, notifications, and widget support. Firebase
services back the real-time product seams, while local repositories and tests
make each feature state easier to validate.
