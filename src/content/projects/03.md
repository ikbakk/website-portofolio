The tradeoff: no design system. Three surfaces, three scopes, the system
came after the launch when the team grew.

Doing it again I would plant a token file from day one even if it is
just CSS custom properties. The product shipped on a small hosting
budget with Core Web Vitals in the green and accessibility at 100.
