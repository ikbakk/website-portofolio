---

The constraint that shaped it: a tourism brand needs to be fast,
discoverable, and easy to extend as new packages appear.

I built the marketing and content surface with Astro, focusing on
technical SEO (Schema.org per package, XML sitemap, canonical URLs,
Open Graph), multilingual routing (Indonesian and Arabic), WebP image
delivery, and a content model the operations team can grow without
touching code. Core Web Vitals stayed in the green across the popular
package pages.
