title: "Design system rollout across six product surfaces"
---

The constraint that shaped it: the design system had to retire six
fragmented libraries without forcing any product team to refactor on the
same week they shipped a launch.

The token taxonomy was split into *primitive*, *semantic*, and
*component* layers so each team could adopt at the layer that fit their
risk tolerance. The release cycle dropped from weeks to days once the
playbook landed.
