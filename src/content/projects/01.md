title: "Design system rollout across six product surfaces"
---

A two-quarter consolidation across B2B and B2C surfaces. Designed the token
taxonomy with the design lead, shipped the primitives, then wrote the
migration playbook so five product teams could move without freezing their
roadmaps.

The constraint that shaped it: the design system had to retire six
fragmented libraries without forcing any product team to refactor on the
same week they shipped a launch. The token taxonomy was split into
*primitive*, *semantic*, and *component* layers so each team could adopt
at the layer that fit their risk tolerance.
