title: 'Ward Monitor — ESP32 patient telemetry dashboard'
---

The constraint that shaped it: a monitoring screen has to be calm enough for
routine ward checks, but loud enough to surface warning, critical, offline, and
unknown states without forcing nurses to read every label.

I built an Angular dashboard around ESP32 telemetry, mocked clinical data, and
backend-ready authentication. The interface groups beds by ward and room, uses
state-tinted cards for quick scanning, keeps vitals compact with icon-led
readings, and includes patient, room, and ward detail routes for the future API
integration.
